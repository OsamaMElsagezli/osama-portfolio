EcoSort - Educational Recycling Game

EcoSort is an educational recycling game developed using Unity and C#. The game teaches environmental awareness by challenging players to sort different waste items into the correct recycling bins while managing score, time limits, and plant health.

Category: Game Development

Tools: Unity, C#, UI Design, Audio Systems, Game Mechanics

Role: Game development, gameplay systems implementation, UI integration, scoring mechanics, and player interaction systems

Project Features

  • Interactive drag-and-drop recycling gameplay
  • Multiple recycling categories and difficulty levels
  • Score tracking and accuracy calculations
  • Plant health monitoring system
  • Timer-based challenges
  • Audio and vibration feedback systems
  • Pause, settings, and results screens
  • Educational focus on recycling and environmental awareness

Technical Highlights

  • GameManager architecture for gameplay control
  • Item spawning and object lifecycle management
  • Drag-and-drop interaction system
  • Scene transition and loading management
  • Statistics tracking and result reporting
  • Audio and vibration management systems
